Sugar Scubs

Why would I want to scrub my skin with coffee?

  • The caffeine found in coffee increases blood flow and helps reduce cellulite
  • The caffeine works to make blood vessels tighter and firmer helping give your skin a firmer appearance
  • The scrub exfoliates your skin removing residual dirt and dead skin cells giving you healthier, glowing skin
  • The antioxidants neutralize the effects of free radicals and can help prevent your skin from premature aging
